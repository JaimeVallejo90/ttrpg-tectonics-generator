Plate Tectonics Generator

Build an A4 point cloud, sketch plates, and layer crust, directions, arrows, cratons, and volcano hotspots in a clear step order.

Quick tips
  • In Paint Boundaries, right click deletes in Boundary mode and resets style in other boundary modes.
  • Repeating a rolled coordinate converts that point into a volcano hotspot marker.
  • Use Move icon to drag markers. Delete removes the selected marker or craton.
